About Me

I'm a third-year Computer Science student at Caraga State University. This site is a space for me to document my learning journey and showcase my projects (and because it's for my ITE 18 class hehe).

On my free time, I run a small Discord community of Tabletop RPG enthusiasts. There we play games like Dungeons & Dragons, Call of Cthulhu, and other TTRPGs. We also cultivate a space for beginners to learn these systems and for artists to share their talents. I also do various types of digital media to support my hobbies (video editing, image editing/digital posters, audio processing, etc.) and upload them throughout my various accounts online.

Here on this site, I'm currently exploring HTML, CSS, and JavaScript fundamentals by crafting simple projects. You can reach me through my contacts below, and you can see my code on Github.
